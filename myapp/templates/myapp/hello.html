Academic Project

GrihoSikkhok – An android app of Finding Tutor with Reliable & Authentic Source

  • GrihoShikkhok is an educational platform that allows students to find their desired tutor and tutors to find tuitions in the most convenient way.
  • The app also proves to be a great platform for skilled and experienced tutors to earn extra income by educating students in their leisure time.
  • Students can choose their interest field and communicate directly with tutors through the inbuilt messaging system or call them directly.
  • GrihoShikkhok app allows easy and secure login by OTP verification system. In GrihoShikkhok app we have implemented KYC system which helps to verify user’s identity and authenticity to avoid fake identities and ensure authenticity.

Technologies used:

  • Java
  • Android Studio
